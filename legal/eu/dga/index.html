DPV-DGA extends the [[[DPV]]] to provide taxonomies of concepts such as legal bases, rights, other relevant concepts as defined within the [[[DGA]]].

The canonical URL for DPV-DGA is https://w3id.org/dpv/dpv-dga which contains (this) specification. The namespace for DPV terms is https://w3id.org/dpv/dpv-dga#, the suggested prefix is dpv-dga, and this document along with source and releases are available at https://github.com/w3c/dpv.

This work is based on the article "Semantics for Implementing Data Reuse and Altruism under EU's Data Governance Act" by Beatriz Esteves, Victor Rodriguez Doncel, Harshvardhan J. Pandit, and Dave Lewis, accepted for presentation at SEMANTiCS 2023. Preprint is here.

Rights under DGA

DGA provides several rights to the data subject, whose applicability depends on the context and nature of processing taking place. DPV lists these rights at an abstract level as concepts along with their origin in specific clauses of the DGA.

In addition to DPV's concepts regarding exercise of rights, DPV-DGA provides additional concepts specific to the implementation of its rights.

  • eu-dga:RightToDataConversionOptOut: Right of data subjects and data holders to opt-out of data conversions e.g. enhance interoperability or harmonisation with standards go to full definition
  • eu-dga:RightToImpartialReview: Right of data subjects and data holders to get an review by an impartial body with the appropriate expertise go to full definition
  • eu-dga:RightToLodgeComplaint: Right of data subjects and data holders to lodge a complaint go to full definition
