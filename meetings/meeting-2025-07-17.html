AOB

JanLindquist: EUDI wallet implementing 27560 and access control. Level of assurance regarding identity – can the DPV provide this?

BeatrizEsteves: Embedded policies and policy controls and agents (e.g. Solid Pods, Wallets) Alternatives: ODRL – allows for more complex policies + there’s multiple implementations of ODRL evaluators now; DCQL – Digital Credentials Query Language (DCQL) - Basically does role-based access control only. If we compare it to Solid’s AC mechanisms, it does basically the same.

Discussion on topics regarding EUDI wallets and the assurance level taxonomy. Suggestion to look if such a taxonomy exists elsewhere, and if not whether DPV should create one. If it exists, does it only require being integrated into DPV? Also good practice to see how this aligns with canonical standards and specs (like mDriving license work in ISO and elsewhere). But otherwise this is in scope of DPV as and where it would be the personal data of the person.
