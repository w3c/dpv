Generic Places

<ghurlbot> Issue 384 Modelling Generic Locations/Places (e.g. Airport, School) (by coolharsh55)

JulianFlake: any news on this topic?

ArthitSuriyawongkul: Summarizes subjective locations and categories of non-subjective locations and distinguishes them from physical locations.

JulianFlake: AtHome, AtWork could/should become part of PD extension, some other concepts (school, train, airport) could/should become part of a specific sector extension

ArthitSuriyawongkul: School, airport, hospital are "abstract" (or logical). School and hospital could be subjective ("my school" and "my hospital", for a regular patient), but airport is not so subjective. Implications of expressing logical locations should be taken into considerations since they can reveal personal data. For example, the fact that a person visit a particular clinic chain/brand (not necessarily a physical location) may reveal the person's religion or association with a trade union in a country that the primary healthcare service or health insurance tend to offered by religious-organization or trade-union.

JulianFlake: First thing is to decide, whether we want to include such concepts now or if we can find a betterfoundation for / existing source of concepts to base on or to connect with.

ChristianDraeger: intuitively I'd think, there exists some vocabulary, somewhere.
