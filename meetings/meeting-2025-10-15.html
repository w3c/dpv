Proposal from Beatriz to map EU Unfair Consumer Directive

<ghurlbot> Issue 392 Extension for the Unfair Contract Terms Directive (by besteves4)

Discussed this as the Directive providing concepts more focused on consumer protection (contract law), though there are some helpful concepts that could be of relevance to DPV. For example, we have contract as a legal basis, and contractual relations and terms also affect privacy and data protection. Therefore, from a consumer protection perspective, what vocabulary should be provide here? Harsh suggested guidelines for Directive to get examples of these.

BeatrizEsteves: goal of this proposal is just to establish if terms connected to personal data protection are in scope of DPV or an extension to DPV. I think most of the work on modelling contractual agreements will be done on an ODRL profile, so the goal is not to have those terms that are not connected to personal data in DPV.

HarshPandit: majority of terms on the directive are not directly connected with data protection; only the ones that are related to this should be in scope of DPV - these would include more specific types of notices, contracts, organisational measures, etc. Cristiana Santos also has a recent paper on this directive and its alignment with the DSA, that looks at dark patterns, which should be considered.
