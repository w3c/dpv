AI Training

<ghurlbot> Issue 82 Provide vocabulary to specify purposes and permissions related to AI training (by scottkellum)

note from harsh: I am not satisfied with these. My issues are 1) how to link training to data processing in DPV; 2) how to separate techniques that may be used in training but are also applicable outside of training; and 3) how to link training to generation of the model.

note from harsh: The current thought process I have goes like this: ai:Train extends dpv:Use to state training is a type of processing (it always includes data, and train can refer to model or system). Then do not have any further distinction between training techniques and AI techniques at this point -- we need an "AI expert" to go over this list and classify them. I find there is a lot of confusion and misinterpretation of "using technique" to actually mean "using model trained using technique". So we don't do this classification at this point -- our technique taxonomy is decent as it is. To state how training was done, a process can state ai:hasTechnique xyz (this was Mayra's point I think). To state data is being used in training dpv:hasProcessing ai:Train is nice and simple.

group: Have ai:Train extending dpv:Use sounds good, we should also consider other processing operation like (model) validating and so on.

Comment from harsh after meeting: validation is a broader process that includes things other than operation on data, therefore it should not be of type dpv:Processing. Training is about manipulation of data specifically, so by definition it fits the processing concept.

group: Should we separate AI and training techniques? At this point probably it is not needed since we donâ€™t have enough expertise yet to make the distinction.
