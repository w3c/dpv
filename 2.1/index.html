DPV 2.1

Summary

We’re pleased to present as the next version of DPV following last year’s DPV 2.0.

The changelog is at https://w3id.org/dpv/2.1/changelog.html. There are no major breaking changes or incompatibilities between 2.1 and 2.0. Specific potential and minor breaking changes are mentioned in the changelog for each specification. In total, DPV 2.1 and all its extensions contain 9167 concepts, and compared to 2.0 - 6553 concepts have been added and 133 concepts removed. Going ahead, we do not foresee major breaking changes in the future release which is tentatively labelled 2.2. Work on the next version has been started under the label 2.2-dev.
