Abstract

This document describes (only) the OWL serialisation of Education Sector Concepts. For information on the concepts, their usage, and further documentation, please consult the canonical page for the vocabulary at: https://w3id.org/dpv/sector/education

Status of This Document

This specification was published by the Data Privacy Vocabularies and Controls Community Group. It is not a W3C Standard nor is it on the W3C Standards Track.
