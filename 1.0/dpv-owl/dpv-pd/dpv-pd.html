DPVS-PD extends the [[[DPV-SKOS]]] to provide categories of personal data. The canonical URL for DPVO-PD is https://w3id.org/dpv-owl/dpv-pd which contains (this) specification. The namespace for DPV terms is https://w3id.org/dpv/dpv-owl/dpv-pd#, the suggested prefix is dpvo-pd, and this document along with source and releases are available at https://github.com/w3c/dpv.
